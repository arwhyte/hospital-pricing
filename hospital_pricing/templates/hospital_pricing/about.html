About US Hospitals

This site combines CMS data on the average amount different U.S. hospitals charge when billing for Diagnosis Related Code (DRG) 101: Seizures without major complications, 203: Bronchitis & Asthma without major complications, and 313: Chest Pain country codes with the CMS hospital quality scores. These scores are on a scale of 1 to 5 with 1 being the lowest overall quality. Heritage Site data. UNESCO World Heritage
